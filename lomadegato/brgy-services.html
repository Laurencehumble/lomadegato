    Obtaining Our Barangay Services

    Step

    01

    Visit the Barangay Office

    Go to the barangay hall or office during their operating hours. You may need to check the specific office hours beforehand.

    Step

    02

    Request an Application Form

    Approach the designated personnel and ask for an application form for the barangay clearance. They may provide you with a printed form or direct you to an online application.

    Step

    03

    Fill out the Application Form

    Carefully complete the application form, providing accurate information about yourself, such as your full name, address, contact details, and purpose for obtaining the clearance.

    Step

    04

    Prepare Supporting Documents

    Gather the required supporting documents, such as a valid identification card (e.g., passport, driver's license),proof of residency (e.g., utility bill, barangay ID), and any other documents specified by the barangay.

    Step

    05

    Submit the Application and Documents

    Return the completed application form along with the supporting documents to the barangay office. The staff will review the documents for completeness and authenticity.

    Step

    06

    Pay the Fee

    You will be required to pay the specified amount. The fee can typically range from Php 20 to Php 500, depending on the requested barangay certificates.

    Step

    07

    Wait for Processing

    The barangay personnel will process your application and conduct the necessary background checks. This may involve verifying your residency, conducting interviews, or cross-checking records.

    Step

    08

    Receive the Barangay Clearance

    Once your application is approved, you will be issued the barangay clearance. The certificate may include details such as your name, address, purpose, and the issuing date.
